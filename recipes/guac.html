Guacamole Recipe

Description

This is so easy and so good. It's always the hit of the party and it's gone before anything else on the table.

Ingredients

  • 5 Avacados
  • Lemon Juice
  • Green Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice in a serving bowl; add the green onion and cilantro; mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning.
