Lasagna Recipe

Description

Start with a layer of lasagna noodles in the bottom of the baking dish, then layer in the cottage cheese mixture, sliced mozzarella, and finally, the meat sauce. Repeat these layers once, then sprinkle parmesan right on top.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • Noodes
  • Ricotta Mixture
  • Mozerella Slices
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Bake the lasagna for an hour.
  5. Cool the lasagna for 15 minutes.
